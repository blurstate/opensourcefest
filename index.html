Rage Against IE

Known as much for their dislike of Internet Explorer as for their music, Rage Against IE helped lay the groundwork for the IE-aggression-fueled rock-rap genre.

Top Songs:

Johnny Padding and the Margins

Johnny got discovered his love for music while volunteering with developers without borders, and has since joined forces with the Margins to pioneer the funky, hip-hop sound we know and love them for today.

Top Songs:

  1. Top, Right, Bottom, Left
  2. Responsively Responsive
  3. CSS is for Cool
